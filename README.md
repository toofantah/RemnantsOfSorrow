# RemnantsOfSorrow
 Remnants of Sorrow is an action-adventure game set in a dystopian future where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to a mysterious disease. The player takes on the role of a member of an ancient race of powerful beings with the ability to shape-shift into various forms with unique abilities.  As the player character, the player embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the disease and find a way to save humanity. Along the way, the player must navigate treacherous terrain, defeat deadly creatures, and solve challenging puzzles.  The game's story is dark and complex, exploring themes of morality, the human condition, and the consequences of one's actions. As the player progresses through the game, they will encounter choices that will have an impact on the story, the world, and the characters they interact with. The morality system tracks the player's choices and actions, determining their alignment towards either a heroic or villainous path. The player's choices ultimately determine the fate of humanity and the protagonist's own fate.  The game features a wide range of puzzle types, including environmental puzzles, logic puzzles, movement puzzles, and more. The player's shape-shifting abilities play a central role in solving puzzles and navigating the game's diverse environments.  Combat in Remnants of Sorrow is fast-paced and action-packed, with the player's abilities changing depending on which form they are currently in. The player can unlock new abilities and weapons throughout the game, and must strategically use them to defeat powerful bosses and overcome difficult challenges.  The game's visuals are stunning, with detailed environments, intricate character designs, and a rich, atmospheric soundtrack. The game's dark and complex story is brought to life through compelling dialogue and intricate cutscenes.  Remnants of Sorrow is an immersive and engaging action-adventure game that offers a unique and thought-provoking experience. With its deep, complex story, challenging puzzles, and intense combat, it is sure to keep players engaged and on the edge of their seats.
